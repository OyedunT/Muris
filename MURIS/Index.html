    About Us

    MURIS Consults Nigeria Limited was established and registered with the aim of providing a modern surveying experience. Founded and managed by a Registered Surveyor, our knowledge and years of experience makes MURIS Consults the professional choice for your surveying projects.

    Our professional surveying services are offered to private, commercial and retail clients as well. We intend to become one of the most dependable source for surveying, always maintaining professionalism as our top priority. We are fully into all aspects of land surveying and hydrographical surveys which includes but not limited to Cadastral surveys – Boundary surveys, Topographical surveys, Layout surveys, Engineering surveys, GIS Consultancy.

    MURIS Consults has experience in fast tract building and site work layout and has a proven track record for maintaining the high accuracy required for any type of surveying. For the past several years in the industry, we have completed successfully projects comprising of different aspects of surveying, working hand in hand with various consultancy firms and other professionals in the built industry. We have extensive surveying equipment at our disposal with well experienced workforce. We are fully dedicated and committed to execute all surveying works perfectly adhering to the survey rules and regulations of the profession on scheduled time and always ensuring that the clients’ requirements are carried out satisfactorily.

    Services

    SURVEYING

    Surveying or land surveying is the technique, profession, and science of determining the terrestrial or three-dimensional positions of points and the distances and angles between them.

    ENGINEERING

    Engineering Surveying is the broad term used to describe the work of surveyors on civil engineering jobs. The role of an engineering surveyor is a lot larger than simply set out for construction of a structure and survey pick-ups.

    GIS CONSULTANTS

    Geographic Information Systems (GIS) consultants are responsible for managing digital mapping data and performing analyses to answer geographical questions.

    PROJECT MANAGEMENT

    Project management is the practice of initiating, planning, executing, controlling, and closing the work of a team to achieve specific goals and meet specific success criteria at the specified time.
